About Us

CosmicLabs is a game-changing non-profit community science center opening soon in South Los Angeles. We're dedicated to inspiring and motivating youth in underserved communities to pursue their dreams in science and the arts through STEAM education.

Our Mission

We aim to create a hub for the community, fostering a safe and supportive environment for youth to learn and explore. CosmicLabs offers various programs and events, including workshops, classes, and camps, designed to ignite passions in STEAM subjects and provide the skills and knowledge to excel in these fields.

Breaking Barriers

CosmicLabs is wholly committed to breaking down barriers to access in STEAM fields by providing necessary resources and opportunities. We are a critical resource for the community, promoting diversity and inclusivity in STEAM fields to create a more equitable and just society.

Join Us

CosmicLabs is not just a vision of the future, it is happening right now. We are already empowering the next generation of scientists, artists, and leaders to break new ground and achieve their goals.
